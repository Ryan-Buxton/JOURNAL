Coded Bias

In the documentary Coded Bias, we learned how coding and artificial intelligence affects our everyday life. From the bias programmed into facial recognition, which due to the data sets they were fed could not as accurately recognize the faces of people of color as they could their white counterparts. These systems are also used as ways of monitoring populations. Not just in China which has become infamous for its use of these technologies in its social credit system, which monitors people's behaviors and uses it to create a credit system which could affect things like bank loans, but also its use in European nations. London has been using facial recognition on its citizens in an attempt to catch criminals, with systems that often misidentify people. Additionally how Paris is trying to implement similar technology leading up to the Olympic games.
